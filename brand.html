About Me

Hello, my name is Kiitarie! I'm a variety streamer who loves playing games, chatting with my community, and exploring new titles. You'll often find me playing cozy games, some competitive shooters, or just hanging out and talking about life. The most important thing to me is building a fun, positive, and inclusive space for everyone.

When I'm not streaming, I enjoy cooking, reading fantasy novels, and spending time with my two cats. I'm so happy to have you here, and I can't wait to get to know you better.
